### Organizations

**Organizations** are the top-level containers for all your work. They represent your company or large organizational unit.

**Creating an Organization:**
1. Click the **organization dropdown** in the top-left
2. Select **"Create Organization"**
3. Enter your **organization name** and **domain**
4. Choose your **subscription plan**
5. **Invite team members** via email

**Organization Settings:**
- **General**: Name, domain, logo, and basic settings
- **Members**: Manage team members and their roles
- **Security**: SSO, domain restrictions, and security policies
- **Billing**: Subscription, usage, and payment methods
- **Integrations**: Connect third-party tools

### Workspaces

**Workspaces** organize related projects and teams within your organization.

**Creating a Workspace:**
1. From any page, click **"+ New Workspace"**
2. Enter a **name** and **description**
3. Choose a **color** for easy identification
4. Set **privacy settings** (Public or Private)
5. **Add team members** and assign roles

**Workspace Roles:**
- **Admin**: Full control over workspace settings and members
- **Member**: Can create and edit boards, manage assigned items
- **Viewer**: Read-only access to workspace content
- **Guest**: Limited access to specific boards only

**Workspace Features:**
- **Dashboard**: Overview of all boards and activity
- **Activity Feed**: Real-time updates from all workspace boards
- **Member Directory**: View all workspace members
- **Templates**: Shared board templates for the workspace
- **Integrations**: Workspace-specific tool connections

### Understanding Boards

**Boards** are flexible containers for organizing work items. Think of them as project dashboards that can be customized for any workflow.

### Creating Your First Board

1. **Navigate** to your workspace
2. Click **"+ New Board"**
3. Choose from options:
   - **Start from Scratch**: Build a custom board
   - **Use Template**: Select from pre-built templates
   - **Import**: Import from other tools

**Popular Templates:**
- **Project Management**: Track tasks, deadlines, and progress
- **Bug Tracking**: Manage software issues and fixes
- **Content Calendar**: Plan and schedule content
- **HR Onboarding**: Employee onboarding checklist
- **Event Planning**: Organize events and activities

### Board Customization

**Columns (Fields)**
Customize columns to match your workflow:

| Column Type | Description | Use Cases |
|-------------|-------------|-----------|
| **Text** | Free-form text input | Names, descriptions, notes |
| **Status** | Dropdown with color-coded options | Progress tracking, phases |
| **People** | Assign team members | Task ownership, collaboration |

1. Click **"+ Add Column"** at the top of your board

4. **Define actions**: Move item to "Completed" group
5. **Test and activate** the rule

## ðŸ’¡ Tips & Best Practices

### Getting Started Tips

**Week 1: Foundation**
- Set up your organization and workspace structure

- Train your team on collaboration features

- Set up notification preferences
- Create shared templates and processes

**Week 4: Optimization**
- Review and refine your workflows
- Set up analytics and reporting
- Create advanced automations
- Plan for scaling to more teams

### Organization Best Practices

**Board Structure**
- **One board per project** or process
- **Use templates** for consistency
- **Keep boards focused** - don't try to do everything in one board
- **Archive completed** boards to reduce clutter

**Naming Conventions**
- **Be descriptive**: "Q1 2025 Product Roadmap" vs "Project 1"
- **Use prefixes**: "[URGENT]", "[DRAFT]", "[COMPLETE]"
- **Stay consistent** across your organization
- **Include dates** when relevant

**Status Management**
- **Keep it simple**: 3-5 status options maximum
- **Use clear labels**: "To Do", "In Progress", "Review", "Done"
- **Color code logically**: Red for urgent, green for complete
- **Define criteria** for each status

### Team Collaboration

**Communication Guidelines**
- **Use @mentions** for urgent requests
- **Comment on items** instead of separate messages
- **Update status regularly** to keep everyone informed
- **Use descriptions** to provide context

**Meeting Management**
- **Create agenda items** as board items
- **Assign action items** during meetings
- **Set due dates** for follow-ups
- **Share meeting boards** with all attendees

**File Organization**
- **Use consistent naming** for uploaded files
- **Create folder structures** in file columns
- **Version control** important documents
- **Archive old files** regularly

### Performance Optimization

**Board Performance**
- **Limit items per board** (< 1000 for best performance)
- **Use filters** instead of scrolling through long lists
- **Archive completed items** regularly
- **Optimize automations** to avoid conflicts

**Notification Management**
- **Customize notifications** to avoid overwhelm
- **Use digest emails** instead of individual notifications
- **Set quiet hours** for non-urgent notifications
- **Unsubscribe** from boards you don't actively use

### Security Best Practices

**Access Control**
- **Review permissions** regularly
- **Use guest access** for external collaborators
- **Remove inactive users** promptly
- **Implement SSO** for larger organizations

**Data Protection**
- **Regular backups** of important boards
- **Avoid sensitive data** in item names (use descriptions)
- **Use private boards** for confidential projects
- **Train team** on data handling policies

## â“ Frequently Asked Questions

### General Questions

**Q: How many workspaces can I create?**
A: Free plans include 3 workspaces. Pro and Enterprise plans have unlimited workspaces.

**Q: Can I use Sunday.com offline?**
A: Yes! The mobile app works offline and syncs when you reconnect to the internet.

**Q: How do I migrate from another tool?**
A: We provide migration tools for popular platforms like Asana, Trello, and Monday.com. Contact support for assistance.

### Technical Questions

**Q: What browsers are supported?**
A: Chrome, Firefox, Safari, and Edge (latest 2 versions of each).

**Q: Is my data secure?**
A: Yes! We use enterprise-grade security including encryption, SOC 2 compliance, and regular security audits.

**Q: Can I export my data?**
A: Absolutely. You can export boards to CSV, Excel, or use our API for custom exports.

### Troubleshooting

**Q: Items aren't updating in real-time**
A: Check your internet connection and refresh the page. If issues persist, contact support.

**Q: I can't find a board I created**
A: Check if you're in the right workspace, or use the global search (Cmd/Ctrl + K).

**Q: Notifications aren't working**
A: Check your notification settings in your profile and browser notification permissions.
