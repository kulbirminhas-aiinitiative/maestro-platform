## Welcome to Maestro

Maestro is an **AI-orchestrated software delivery platform** that transforms how software is conceived, designed, built, tested, and deployed. Leveraging intelligent agents, dependency-driven execution, and comprehensive quality assurance, Maestro delivers **10x improvement in development velocity** with enterprise-grade quality.

> **Vision:** "Democratize enterprise software delivery through intelligent AI orchestration, enabling teams of any size to deliver software at the velocity and quality of world-class engineering organizations."

## Core Principles

1. **User-First Design** - Intuitive experiences
2. **Intelligence Over Automation** - Context-aware decisions
3. **Quality as Foundation** - Tri-modal assurance (DDE + BDV + ACC)
4. **Human-AI Collaboration** - Augment, don't replace
5. **Provider Agnostic** - Multi-LLM support
6. **Contract-First** - Interfaces before implementation
